Bubblegum Shooters

A Custom Cocktail Recipe By Ian Sullivan

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Fireball Whiskey
  • 2 oz Pinnacle Whipped Vodka
  • 4 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 2 oz Orange Juice
  • 1 oz Grenadine May Substitute Cherry Syrup
  • 1 cup Cubed Ice

Directions

  1. fill shaker 3/4 full with cubed ice(Use a couple of odd sized glasses if no shaker can be found)
  2. Pour the Orange Juice and Pineapple Juice into the shaker
  3. Pour the Fireball Whiskey and Pinnacle Whipped vodka into the shaker
  4. Add the Grenadine
  5. Close shaker and shake it like it owes you money
  6. Strain into 4-6 shot glasses
  7. Share!

Blondie

Ingredients

  • 1/2oz Rumple Minze
  • 1/2oz Goldschlager
  • 1/4oz Tuaca

Directions

  1. Fill shaker 3/4 full with cubed ice(Use a couple of odd sized glasses if no shaker can be found)
  2. Add Rumple Minze and Goldschlager to shaker.
  3. Shake it like Michael J. Fox
  4. Strain into a shot glass
  5. Gently pour the Tuaca against side of shot glass to float it on top of the blend

Pink Pussy

Ingredients

  • 3/4oz Butterscotch Schnapps
  • 1/2oz Tequila Rose

Directions

  1. Pour Butterscotch Schnapps into shot glass
  2. Gently pour Tequila Rose against side of shot glass to float it on top of the Butterscoth Schnapps
  3. If the two liqueurs blend, set in freezer for 5-10 minutes for it to separate

Cinnamon Toast Crunch

Ingredients

  • 1/2oz Rum Chata
  • 1/2oz Tequila Rose
  • 1/2oz Captain Morgan
  • 1/2oz Blue Curacao
  • 1/2oz Half and Half

Directions

  1. Fill shaker 3/4 full with cubed ice(Use a couple of odd sized glasses if no shaker can be found)
  2. Add liquors and half and half
  3. Shake it like a British nanny
  4. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass

Liquid Marijuana

    Ingredients

  • 1/2oz Spiced Rum
  • 1/2oz Coconut Rum
  • 1/2oz Blue Curacao
  • 1/2oz Melon Liqueur
  • 2oz Pineapple Juice
  • Splash of Orange Juice

Directions

  1. fill shaker 3/4 full with cubed ice(Use a couple of odd sized glasses if no shaker can be found)
  2. Add Pineapple and Orange Juices
  3. Add all liquors
  4. close shaker tin
  5. Shake it like it owes you money
  6. Strain into hurricane glass full of fresh ice
